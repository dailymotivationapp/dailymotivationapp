Privacy Policy for Daily Motivation

Effective Date:April 20, 2026
Last Updated:April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Daily Motivation (the "App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. Your privacy is important to us, and we have designed this App to minimize data collection.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Daily Motivation does not require you to create an account. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, date of birth, or any other personally identifying information. We do not have servers that store your data.

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information only on your device. This information is never transmitted to us or any third party by the App:

  • Your saved favorite quotes
  • Your preferred settings (voice selection, speech rate, pitch, notification time)
  • Your notification preferences

This data is managed using the Android SharedPreferences system. If you uninstall the App or clear its storage, all of this data is permanently removed from your device.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob is a service provided by Google LLC. When ads are served, Google and its advertising partners may collect and process information about you, which may include:

  • Your device's advertising identifier (a resettable ID provided by the Android operating system)
  • Information about your device (model, operating system version, language)
  • Approximate (non-precise) location, inferred from your IP address
  • Information about your interactions with ads (impressions, clicks)

This information is used to serve and measure advertisements, including personalized advertisements where permitted. Google's use of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (or Opt out of Ads Personalization)

3.2 Google Play Services

The App uses Google Play Services to support notifications and ad delivery. Google Play Services is subject to the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Notifications

The App can send you local notifications — for example, a daily quote at a time you choose. These notifications are generated entirely on your device using Android's local notification system. We do not operate a push-notification server, and no data about the notification schedule or its content leaves your device.

5. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — required to send you daily quote notifications
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM — required to deliver notifications at your chosen time
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — required to restore scheduled notifications after your device restarts
  • WAKE_LOCK — required to keep the screen awake during the sleep timer feature
  • VIBRATE — required for notification alerts
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS — optional, requested only if you choose to enable reliable background notifications
  • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — required by Google AdMob to serve ads

6. Children's Privacy

Daily Motivation is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

7.1 Users in the European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete data about you. Because the App itself does not collect personal information, most of these rights can be exercised directly through your device by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling the App. For data processed by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and their user controls.

7.2 Users in California (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). The App does not sell personal information. We do not collect personal information directly, so most CCPA rights apply to the third-party services listed in Section 3 and should be exercised directly with those providers.

8. Data Security

Data stored by the App is kept in your device's application-private storage area, which is accessible only to the App itself. We rely on the security features of the Android operating system to protect this data.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Significant changes will also be communicated through an update to the App.
